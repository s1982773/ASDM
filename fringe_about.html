ABOUT SECTION

Welcome to the Fringe Accessibility Web Map! Here at the festival, we believe that everyone has the right to experience the thrill of live performance.
Nothing should have to stop our visitors with special needs to enjoy the Fringe! To help with this, browse this webpage and try out one of our accessibility
tools to help you plan your journey to the Fringe in the most suitable way.

The Fringe Map

-  Search where in Edinburgh shows and walking tours are on display

The Accessibility Venue Finder

-  Search for venues suitable for specific needs

Distance Calculator

-  Find out the distance of walking tours and integrate these with your travel plans

To give you inspiration, have a look at the profiles below and how these may browse our accessibility tools to plan a visit to the festival.

A Wheelchair User

-  Browse the Venue Finder for venues with wheelchair access
-  Browse the Venue Finder for venues with disabled toilets
-  Browse the Venue Finder for venues within a certain distance of a blue card parking zone

Someone with Strict Dietary Requirements

Browse the Venue Finder for venues with food available

Someone with Walking Difficulties

  • -  Browse the Venue Finder for venues within a certain distance from any transportation hub
  • -  Use the Distance Calculator to calculate the length of walking tours
  • -  Use the Distance Calculator to calculate the length of a walking tour plus the distances to and from your selected transportation hub

Someone with anxiety or high sensitivity to crowds

  • -  Browse the Venue Finder for small sized venues
  • -  Browse the Venue Finder for venues that are not located in crowded parts of Edinburgh
  • -  Browse the Venue Finder for venues that lie within a specified distance of a preferred transport hub
